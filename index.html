Privacy Policy for EZ Grader

Effective Date: April 13, 2026

Thank you for using EZ Grader. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our application.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. EZ Grader is designed to function as a grading calculator. All calculations, grades, and related data entered into the application are stored entirely locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your data on external servers.

2. Use of Information

Since establishing the core functionality of the app relies solely on your device's local storage, there is no information collected by us to be used for marketing, analytics (unless explicitly opted-in via third-party platforms like Google Play), or tracking purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

The app does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Please refer to the privacy policies of third-party service providers (such as Google Play Services or AdMob, if applicable) regarding their data collection practices.

4. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us with your information (which is kept locally). However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
